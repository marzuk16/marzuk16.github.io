About

I've spent five years building and scaling production systems, mostly with Java and Spring Boot across national government platforms serving hundreds of thousands of users. The work I still enjoy most is building things that hold up under real load: stable, observable, and reliable when it actually matters.

I work across the full stack. On most projects I've owned everything from backend microservices and database design to server-side UI, monitoring dashboards, and production debugging. I prefer that kind of end-to-end ownership, it makes the work more interesting and makes me a better collaborator for the people around me.

Outside of the code, I've spent a lot of time mentoring junior developers and leading delivery, translating requirements into architecture, running code reviews, and helping teams ship with confidence. I'm looking for a role where engineering quality is taken seriously and there's real complexity to dig into.
